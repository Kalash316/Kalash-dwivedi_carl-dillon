INSTALLATION STEPS

Step 1 – Locate Wall Studs

• Use a stud finder to identify support beams.
• Mark stud positions with painter's tape.
• Verify measurements before drilling.

---

Step 2 – Determine Mount Height

• Measure viewing position.
• Mark center line for television placement.
• Ensure comfortable eye-level viewing.

---

Step 3 – Install Wall Bracket

• Align mounting bracket with wall studs.
• Use lag bolts for secure installation.
• Confirm bracket is level before tightening.

---

Step 4 – Attach Mounting Arms

• Install VESA mounting arms to rear panel.
• Tighten screws according to manufacturer specifications.
• Verify stability.

---

Step 5 – Mount Television

• Lift television carefully using two people.
• Secure mounting arms to wall bracket.
• Confirm locking mechanism is fully engaged.

---

CABLE MANAGEMENT

• Route HDMI cables through cable covers.
• Bundle power and signal cables separately.
• Leave additional slack for maintenance access.
